Asparagus & halloumi salad

INGREDIENTS:

3 Red Capsicums Olive oil cooking spray (if not available, use regular oil and a brush to spread it on) 1 Cup Green Olives 1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar 1 Tbsp Lemon Juice 1‚ÅÑ4 Cup Basil Leaves, roughly chopped 1‚ÅÑ4 Mint Leaves, roughly chopped 1 Cup of Brussel Sprouts 3 Bunches of Asparagus, trimmed 400 g Halloumi, cut into thick slices Crusty baguette, to serve

METHOD:
  1. Heat a barbecue hotplate on medium-high heat.
  2. Spray capsicum with oil. Cook for 10 minutes or until charred on all sides.
  3. Transfer to a bowl and cover, wait for 5 min to cool. Peel (optional) and remove seeds. Roughly chop and place into a bowl with green olives.
  4. Cook asparagus and brussel sprouts on a barbecue plate for 5 minutes until just tender.
  5. Cook halloumi on a barbecue plate for 1-2 minutes until golden.
  6. Add them to the salad bowl
  7. In a jug whisk oil, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, salt (optional), and pepper, and add to salad. Toss well to combine. Sprinkle it with basil and mint.
  8. Transfer to serving plates. Serve with a crusty baguette.

Roasted chicken & Cauliflower rice

INGREDIENTS:

2 Cloves 3 Whole Grains of Cardamom 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon 1/2 Tsp Ground Black Pepper 1/2 Tsp Salt 1/2 Tsp Turmeric powder 1 Bay leaf 1 Chicken Breast 2 Tsp Olive Oil 1/4 Chopped Onion 1 Cup of chopped Cauliflower Fresh Coriander to decorate. Sauce: 1/2 Tomato 2 Tbsp chopped Coriander 1 Clove of Garlic 1 Hot Pepper (optional) 2 Tbsp Lemon juice 1/4 Tspn Salt

METHOD:
  1. Grind all the spices together until they turn into a fine powder.
  2. Add half of the spice powder to the chicken breast until it is completely covered on both sides. Then, leave it in the refrigerator for two hours, or a whole night.
  3. Add olive oil to a grilling pan and heat it on medium heat. Then add the chicken breast to the pan to toast it on both sides, without completely cooking it.
  4. After that, put the chicken breast in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes, or until it is completely cooked.
  5. Add the chopped onions to the same roasting pan used in Step 3 until they become soft and golden.
  6. Add the ground cauliflower and the rest of the spice powder to the onion and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, until the cauliflower is tender.
  7. To prepare the sauce, place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until a liquid mixture is formed.
  8. Transfer the cauliflower and chicken to a serving plate and serve with the sauce.
